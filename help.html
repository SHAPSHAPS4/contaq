⚡ AI Quote Builder

Uploading your first drawing

Supported file types and best practices

Supported formats: PDF (best results), DWG, scanned drawings.

For best results:

  • Use clear, legible PDFs — not photos of drawings
  • Include the drawing schedule/legend if available
  • GA (General Arrangement) drawings give the best extraction
  • Max file size: 50MB per document

You can upload multiple drawings at once — the AI will process them together.

Understanding confidence scores

What High, Medium, and Low confidence mean

Every extracted item has a confidence level:

  • High — Extracted directly from drawing annotations or matched against the specification. Standard review recommended.
  • Medium — Partially estimated. Some values inferred from industry defaults (BSRIA/BS standards). Check flagged parameters against the project spec.
  • Low — AI estimate requiring verification. Values inferred from industry defaults. Open the source document and verify before including in a tender.

Always review Medium and Low items carefully. The AI flags what it's unsure about.

Reviewing and correcting extractions

How to accept, flag, or reject items

After extraction, review each item:

  • ✓ Accept — item is correct, include in quote
  • ⚑ Flag — include but mark for further review in Quote Book
  • × Reject — remove from quote (hallucinated or out of scope)

Important: Every correction you make teaches the AI. Rejected items become learned rules that prevent the same mistake next time. The more you correct, the smarter it gets for your specific projects.

Creating a quote from extraction

Turning AI-extracted items into a formal quote

  1. Review all items (accept/flag/reject)
  2. Click "Confirm & Create Quote"
  3. The quote appears in your Quote Book
  4. From there you can assign it to a client, edit line items, and track its status

Quote Book overview

Tracking your quotes and tenders

The Quote Book shows all your quotes in one place. Each quote has a status: Draft, Submitted, Won, Lost, or Expired.

Click any quote to see its line items, attached drawings, and history.

Projects and clients

Organising your work

When a quote is won, create a Project from it. Projects track the full lifecycle: from accepted quote through to final account.

How accurate is the AI?

What to expect from AI extractions

Accuracy depends on drawing quality and trade complexity. On clear GA drawings:

  • High confidence items: typically 85-95% accurate
  • Medium confidence: 60-80% — always verify
  • Low confidence: treat as estimates only

The AI gets better over time as your team corrects extractions. After 10+ corrections, accuracy typically improves by 10-15%.

Never submit a tender based solely on AI extraction without human review.
