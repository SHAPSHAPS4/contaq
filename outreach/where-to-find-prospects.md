# Where to Find M&E Subcontractor Prospects

## 1. Trade Associations (member directories)

| Association | Trade Coverage | Website | How to Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **BESA** (Building Engineering Services Association) | All M&E | besa.org.uk | Member directory searchable by trade and region |
| **ECA** (Electrical Contractors' Association) | Electrical | eca.co.uk | Find a contractor tool |
| **SELECT** | Electrical (Scotland) | select.org.uk | Member search |
| **SNIPEF** | Plumbing & heating (Scotland) | snipef.org | Find an installer |
| **APHC** (Association of Plumbing & Heating Contractors) | Plumbing | aphc.co.uk | Find a plumber |
| **FETA** (Federation of Environmental Trade Associations) | HVAC/Refrigeration | feta.co.uk | Member list |
| **TICA** (Thermal Insulation Contractors Association) | Insulation | tica-acad.co.uk | Membership list — your direct competitors and peers |
| **ASFP** (Association for Specialist Fire Protection) | Fire protection | asfp.org.uk | Member directory |

## 2. Approved Supplier Lists

- **Constructionline** (constructionline.co.uk) — search by trade, region, turnover
- **Builders Profile** — many Tier-1s use this for prequalification
- **CHAS** (Contractors Health & Safety Assessment Scheme)
- **Achilles BuildingConfidence** — Tier-1 supply chain portal
- **SafeContractor** — accreditation database

*Tip: Search for "M&E subcontractor" or specific trades like "insulation contractor" filtered by your region.*

## 3. Companies House (SIC Codes)

Search companies-house.gov.uk for these SIC codes:

| SIC Code | Description | Likely Prospects |
|---|---|---|
| 43220 | Plumbing, heat and air-conditioning installation | Plumbing, HVAC contractors |
| 43210 | Electrical installation | Electrical subcontractors |
| 43290 | Other construction installation | Insulation, fire protection, ductwork |
| 43310 | Plastering | Some insulation contractors file here |
| 43999 | Other specialised construction activities | Multi-trade M&E |

*Filter by: active companies, annual turnover £250k-£10M, incorporated in last 10 years.*

## 4. LinkedIn Sales Navigator Filters

**Search criteria:**
- Industry: Construction
- Company size: 2-50 employees
- Keywords: "M&E subcontractor" OR "insulation contractor" OR "electrical subcontractor" OR "mechanical contractor" OR "ductwork" OR "pipework"
- Location: United Kingdom
- Title: Director, Owner, Estimator, Commercial Manager, QS

*Free LinkedIn search works too — just less precise.*

## 5. Main Contractor Approved Lists

Many Tier-1s publish their supply chain or approved subcontractor lists:

- **Balfour Beatty** — supply chain portal
- **Morgan Sindall** — subcontractor registration
- **Mace Group** — supply chain
- **Skanska** — subcontractor portal
- **Kier Group** — supply chain directory
- **BAM** — approved list
- **ISG** — subcontractor registration

*Check their procurement/supply chain pages. Often they list categories including M&E.*

## 8. WhatsApp/Trade Groups

- Ask your existing contacts if they're in any M&E WhatsApp groups
- Facebook groups: "UK Plumbers Forum", "Electricians Chat UK", "Insulation Contractors UK"
- Trade forum sites: plumbersforums.net, diynot.com trade section

## 9. Direct Site Visits

- When you're on site with Foxdon, notice which other M&E subs are there
- Plant room = pipe fitters, insulation, electrical all in one space
- Ask for the commercial manager's email

## 10. Referrals

- After your first 3 beta users are happy, ask them: "Do you know anyone else who might find this useful?"
- Offer: "If you refer another sub who joins the beta, we'll extend your free trial by a month"

---

## Daily Outreach Routine (30 minutes)

1. **Morning (10 min):** Find 3 new prospects from the sources above. Add to tracker.
2. **Midday (10 min):** Send 3 personalised emails using the templates. Log in tracker.
3. **Evening (10 min):** Follow up on any from 4+ days ago. Check LinkedIn replies.

**Target:** 15 outreaches per week → 3-5 responses → 1-2 beta signups.
After 4 weeks: 5-8 active beta users providing real feedback.
