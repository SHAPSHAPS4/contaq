Service Level Agreement

This SLA applies to all Contraq Beta users. It outlines our commitments for uptime, support response times, and data protection.

MetricTarget

Scheduled maintenance windowSundays 02:00-06:00 GMT (with 24hr notice)

DatabaseManaged by Supabase with automated failover

MeasureDetail

Account deletionFull deletion within 7 days of request
Data retention after cancellation90 days (then permanently deleted)

Exclusions

This SLA does not cover:

  • Outages caused by third-party services (Anthropic API, Supabase)
  • Issues caused by user error or unsupported browsers
  • Scheduled maintenance within the published window
  • Force majeure events

Beta Disclaimer

Contraq is currently in beta. While we commit to the targets above, beta software may contain bugs and incomplete features. We actively monitor for issues and deploy fixes rapidly. Your feedback during beta directly improves the platform for everyone.
